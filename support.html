How Memory Watch Works

Memory Watch searches publicly available information — including Wikipedia and the US Social Security Administration — to help you learn more about people from your past.

Search Tips

Enter a first and last name and tap Find Out. If you get too many results, tap + Add more details to add a middle initial, birth year, or suffix to narrow your search.

Watchlist & Alerts

The Watchlist is where you can add a person if no sign of passing is found, or no information is available. Memory Watch will alert you in the app when new public information becomes available.

Memories

When information indicates someone has passed, you can save them to your Memories for future reference.

Account & Data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app under Info → Account Removal.

Information Coverage

Memory Watch uses information from Wikipedia and the US Social Security Administration. Information may not always be complete or up to date.
